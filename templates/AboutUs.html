About us

This competition is brought to you by the INFOSec club, a club formed last year dedicated to talking about (and teaching) information security, or how to keep yourself safe, online.

In addition to info/chat/qna/whatever sessions, we also host regular competitions for fun (such as this one). We are also preparing members for worldwide competitions like picoCTF, an annual Capture The Flag competition hosted by Carnegie Mellon University (because we all need that resume pad :p)

Feel free to hop by any time; sessions are pretty chill and don’t really connect to each other too much (such is the nature of the topic, unfortunately) so you aren’t missing out on much, if anything at all.

Thanks & Enjoy,

Andrew & Tyllis (Club Officers)
