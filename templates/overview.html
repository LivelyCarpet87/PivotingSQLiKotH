Overview

Welcome! This is a King of the Hill(KOTH)-style, Attack-Defense based competition centered around control over a network of devices.

Now, in more plain terms:

King-Of-The-Hill style competitions refer to those where the goal is to be the ‘king’, or the team in control of a particular thing, called the ‘hill’. In our case, the ‘hill’ would refer to the various servers on the game network.

Attack-Defense based competitions refer to those which involve both an attacking aspect(taking control over other peoples’ servers) and a defending aspect(making sure that others don’t take control over your servers).

Taking over a device is done by either logging in with the correct password or logging in without the correct password (hacking!). More explanation on the latter one in the bootcamp section .

A network of devices has been set in place with restrictions to model real-life networks, with features such as scaling point functions(because in real life, being in control of a device for a longer amount of time amounts to more profit for attackers), restricted server access (modeling real network topography), etc.

The ultimate goal of the competition is to rack up the most points by being in control of the most servers for the longest amount of time..

Oh, and of course, to win prizes and have fun. :)
