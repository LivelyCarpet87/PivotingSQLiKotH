About Filters

Filters are crucial in this game. If any field contains an item in the filters, the server will ignore the request. Thus it is very important to make filters to protect input from SQL injection. Be aware if you are blocking database queries though. Filters ignore case and all spaces when comparing. It is highly recommended to filter SQL keywords.

Devices

Initially in the game, your team controls 2 devices. You should defend these nodes to maintain access to the rest of the network and gain valuable points. As shown on the map above, nodes are connected to other nodes in a specific layout, so you must navigate the network to attack high-value targets and defend your connections. Remember that some connections are one way, meaning that the attacked node cannot retaliate. In these cases, try to cut off your opponent as a defensive measure.
